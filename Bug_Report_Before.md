\*\*Device:\*\* Realme RMXXX, Android 13  

\*\*Severity:\*\* Major  

\*\*Priority:\*\* High

1\. Open the app.

App crashes and closes after about 3 seconds.  



\*\*Expected result:\*\*

Happens every time. No crash log.
